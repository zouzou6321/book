# 第128章：万米高空的茶话会
**时间**：飞行途中，时间模糊
**地点**：波音747客舱（旧日回廊内）

那种失重感并没有持续太久，就像是一脚踩空了楼梯，随后却又莫名其妙地踩到了实地。

飞机剧烈颠簸了一下，引擎发出的不再是那种濒死的惨叫，而是一种仿佛老旧唱片机卡带般的、带着节奏感的轰鸣。

“咔哒、咔哒、嗡——”

李疯狂眨了眨眼。

驾驶舱舷窗外的景象变了。原本灰暗混沌、吞噬一切的巨口消失不见，取而代之的是一片令人心悸的“湛蓝”。那是只有他在第一卷以前的记忆里才见过的、属于旧时代正午天空的颜色。阳光明媚得刺眼，云朵像棉花糖一样堆积在机翼下方，洁白、柔软，毫无杂质。

但李疯狂左眼深邃的紫色瞳孔却在微微抽搐。

视野里，那些云朵不是水蒸气，而是由无数张惨白的人脸拼凑而成的；那阳光也不是光子，而是某种高浓度的神经毒素粉末，正在试图透过特种玻璃烧灼出视网膜上的盲点。

“这地方……还真是喜欢搞这种‘怀旧滤镜’啊。”李疯狂低声嘟囔了一句，松开了已经变形的操纵杆。

飞机进入了自动驾驶。准确地说，是被某种不可见的规则接管，平稳得像是在传送带上滑行的包裹。

李疯狂转身看向客舱。

原本阴森恐怖、挂满怨灵血浆的机舱，此刻竟然发生了翻天覆地的变化。破碎的座椅自行愈合，变成了那种令人怀念的、深蓝色的人造革材质；地板上的血迹化为了整洁的地毯，空气中弥漫着一股陈旧的香水味，混合着刚刚冲泡好的廉价咖啡香气。

只有苏浅还躺在原本的位置上。她蜷缩在宽大的航空座椅里，脸色依旧苍白，但呼吸已经平稳。那一半高纯度理智药剂正在她体内发挥作用，像是一道临时的防火墙，暂时隔绝了外界那些乱七八糟的精神污染。

李疯狂走过去，替她将有些滑落的毯子盖好。

手指触碰到毯子的瞬间，那种粗糙的织物感让他稍微找回了一点真实感。

“睡吧。”他轻声说，声音里没有平日那种玩世不恭的冷硬，只有一丝不易察觉的疲惫，“这种无聊的茶话会，不用你操心。”

就在这时，一阵推车的轮轴滚动声，从客舱尽头幽幽传来。

“滚轮滚过地板……咕噜噜……咕噜噜……”

李 crazy slowly straightened up, his left hand subconsciously touching the bone spoon made from Li Xiaoyu's rib hidden in his clothes. The corner of his mouth twitched upward, forming a mocking arc.

"Service is here."

He turned around.

A silver meal cart was slowly pushing over. It was polished bright, reflecting the fake blue sky "outside". Behind the cart stood a stewardess.

She wore the classic red and blue uniform of the old era, her hair styled in an immaculate bun, and her smile was standard—revealing exactly eight teeth.

But from the neck down, her body was semi-transparent, composed of a swarm of wriggling, maggot-like data streams. Her nametag read: *Flight Attendant 404 - Regret*.

"Dear guest, good afternoon." The stewardess's voice sounded like two pieces of rusted metal rubbing together, yet she maintained that sweet tone. "Today's lunch is 'Memory Sashimi'. Would you like a main course of 'Failed Promotion', or a side dish of 'Unrequited Love'?"

The cart stopped in front of Li Crazy.

On the metal tray lay a plate of pitch-black tar, which was still squirming, emitting a sound like weeping.

Li Crazy looked at the "meal" and sneered, "I don't eat leftovers. Especially the kind that has been digested by the world."

"Sir, you must eat." The stewardess's smile stiffened slightly, the eight teeth beginning to loosen and drip a black fluid. "Passengers are strictly forbidden from going hungry in the 'Old Day Corridor'. Hunger represents imperfection, and imperfection needs to be... filled."

As she spoke, two semi-transparent arms stretched out from the cart, trying to grab Li Crazy's head and force the black tar into his mouth.

【Warning! Detected high-dimensional conceptual pollution: 'Forced Feeding'. Logical contamination risk: 35%!】
【Suggestion: Use the 'Tweaker' ability to redefine the用餐规则.】

Li Crazy didn't even blink. He didn't dodge.

He simply raised his hand and snapped his fingers.

【Tamper · Protocol Override: Food Safety Inspection】

"Stop."

The stewardess's movements froze instantly. Her arms were still two inches away from Li Crazy's face, unable to advance an inch.

"Your hygiene permit has expired." Li Crazy looked at her with those indifferent purple eyes, his tone as if he were reviewing a piece of garbage code. "Look at this 'ingredient'." He pointed to the black tar. "Bacteria count exceeded, mental residue exceeded, logic consistency... zero. According to the *Food Hygiene Law of the Mind* version 5.0, this is inedible industrial waste."

He took a step forward, his aura pressing down like a tangible weight.

"As a senior 'Food Hygiene Supervisor' (pseudo), I now issue you a rectification notice."

The stewardess's smile began to distort, her face melting like wax. "Sir... this is not in compliance... rules... hunger..."

"Rules?"

Li Crazy sneered. He reached out and grabbed the "stewardess" by the throat, though he felt only cold data streams. He yanked her close.

"Then let me teach you a new rule."

He glanced at the emergency exit door on the side of the cabin.

【Tamper · Definition Modification: 'Emergency Exit' -> 'Trash Chute'】

"In my jurisdiction, unqualified waste must be disposed of immediately."

Li Crazy pulled down the emergency handle.

*Whoosh—!*

The cabin door did not open to the outside. Instead, a terrifying suction force erupted from the door frame, but it didn't affect the air in the cabin—it only targeted specific targets.

The stewardess screamed, a sound like a modem dialing up. Her semi-transparent body was instantly elongated, torn apart, and then sucked into that black vortex like a piece of confetti.

The silver meal cart trembled, then followed her in, clanking down the "trash chute".

*Squeak—Bang!*

The "door" automatically closed.

The silence returned to the cabin.

Li Crazy dusted off his hands. A look of disgust flashed across his face. "Low-level loop entities. Always thinking they can solve data corruption by stuffing data into it. Don't they know that eating too much causes indigestion?"

He walked back to the food cart's original position.

Although the stewardess and the cart were gone, a small, crumpled object remained on the carpet where the cart had been—as if the vacuum had deliberately filtered it out.

Li Crazy picked it up.

It was a ticket.

An old-fashioned, hard-paper airplane ticket, yellowed with edges, as if it had gone through decades of time.

Li Crazy narrowed his eyes and looked at the contents on the ticket.

**Passenger:** Li Crazy (The Unfinished)
**Flight:** CF404
**Destination:** The Eve of Genesis (Before the First Thursday)
**Seat:** 001 (Observer)
**Status:** Rejected (Waiting for Refund)

"The Eve of Genesis..." Li Crazy rubbed the rough texture of the ticket with his thumb. A strange look surged in his eyes.

"Before the first Thursday... Is that the starting point of everything? Or the archive room before the world went mad?"

He had known for a long time that the "补天计划" required fragments, but he didn't expect the key to be hidden in such a place. The "Old Day Corridor" lived up to its name; it was a garbage dump stacked with failed timelines, but also a warehouse of "original samples".

"Thinking about it, if I can go back to the 'Eve'..."

Li Crazy suddenly froze.

Go back? Change everything? Let the world remain in the boring, peaceful Monday to Friday loop? Let the monsters disappear, let Li Xiaoyu be alive, let Su Qian never suffer the pain of mutation?

Is that... the optimal solution?

His left eye suddenly throbbed, a piercing pain piercing his brain. The purple "V" symbol in his pupil seemed to be splitting, bleeding a tear of black data.

【Warning! Logic engine detecting a 'Paradox Trap'. 'Regret' emotional module is attempting to overwrite the 'Logic' module. Current Sanity: 11%... 10.9%...】

Li Crazy shook his head hard, spitting out a mouthful of cold air.

"Damn it... hallucinations again."

He gripped the ticket tightly, crushing it into a ball in his palm. "There is no 'Eve'. If there was, it would have been eaten long ago. This ticket... is just a bait, or maybe a map to a grave."

He took a deep breath, forcing himself to stop thinking about those what-ifs.

He turned to look at Su Qian again.

Su Qian seemed to sense something. Her eyelashes trembled, and she slowly opened her eyes. Her left eye, the pitch-black vortex, was now quiet, with only a faint crimson data stream circling the pupil.

"What... happened?" Su Qian's voice was hoarse. She struggled to sit up. "I seem to have heard... screaming?"

"Just a little turbulence. A flight attendant who didn't follow the rules fell out of the plane." Li Crazy spoke lightly, concealing the dangerous concept pollution.

He sat down in the seat next to her and leaned back. The tension in his nerves didn't relax, but his posture seemed a bit lazy.

"Rest for a while. We still have a long way to go."

"Where are we going?" Su Qian looked at him, then at the "blue sky" outside the window. Her intuition told her that this beautiful scenery was the most dangerous trap.

"To take back what was lost." Li Crazy said vaguely.

He closed his eyes, seemingly resting, but his consciousness sank into the system interface.

The system interface was currently filled with red error messages, all reporting about the "Old Day Corridor".

He needed to find the next coordinate. This "plane" wouldn't fly forever. The "Old Day Corridor" was a maze, and every stop was a memory node. If he didn't find the exit before the plane ran out of "fuel", they would become part of this ghost place forever.

Fuel...

Li Crazy suddenly opened his eyes and looked at the instrument panel on the cockpit bulkhead.

The fuel gauge was red.

But the pointer wasn't dropping; it was rising.

It was full.

Weird. The fuel gauge was full, but the "engine temperature" was dropping rapidly. This was logically wrong. Unless... the fuel being used wasn't aviation kerosene, but something else.

He stood up again and walked towards the passenger cabin behind.

The economy class was full of "passengers".

Those were the "filling dolls" he had seen earlier. They sat in their seats, heads bowed, wearing the clothes of various eras, motionless.

Li Crazy walked to a middle-aged man in a suit and reached out to touch his "forehead".

Cold. Hard.

It wasn't a human, nor a corpse. It was a... wax figure? No, it was "Time Crystal".

"Are they... fuel?" Su Qian's voice came from behind. She had walked over, supporting the seat back.

"Yes." Li Crazy's voice was indifferent. "Every 'passenger' here is a fixed 'fragment of time'. They are the memories of this world. The plane is burning these memories to keep flying."

He pointed to the safety cards in the seat pockets in front of the passengers. They were all blank.

"They have no destination, no past, no future. They are stuck here forever, burning themselves to maintain this absurd 'flight'."

Su Qian felt a chill. "Then us..."

"We are 'stowaways'." Li Crazy turned his head, a purple glint in his eyes. "We have tickets, but we aren't fuel. To the plane, we are the 'indelible impurities'."

"Indelible?"

"Because we are still 'changing', still 'alive'. Dead memories are the best fuel, but living, changing will is bad gasoline. It will ruin the engine."

Li Crazy sneered. "So sooner or later, the 'Captain' of this plane will notice us and try to 'clean' the engine."

As if to confirm his words, the plane suddenly shook violently.

The "seatbelt" sign flashed with a "Danger" red light.

The broadcasting system rang again, still that childlike, cheerful voice, but this time, with a distinct electronic glitch:

"Attention passengers. Attention passengers. We detected that there are two 'rats' without safety belts in the cargo hold. The 'purser' is very angry. Purser is very angry. Punishment mode: Vacuum. Time remaining: 300 seconds. Please prepare to be... tidied up."

The cabin walls began to seep a black mist. The "passengers" in the seats started to tilt their heads one by one, their necks making cracking sounds, staring at Li Crazy and Su Qian with hollow eye sockets.

"看来不用等了。" Li Crazy's eyebrows shot up. "这机长还是个急性子。"

He glanced at Su Qian. "Hold onto something tight. This might get bumpy. I need to 'upgrade' this flight."

He pulled the bone spoon from his waist.

【Tamper · Property Modification: 'Plastic Bone Spoon' -> 'Titanium Alloy Crowbar'】

"Ready?"

Su Qian gritted her teeth, her left eye's black vortex spinning rapidly. She pulled out a dagger from her waist, her body radiating a faint, cold light.

"Always."

Li Crazy laughed, then suddenly slammed the bone spoon into the ceiling of the cabin!

【Tamper · Concept Rewrite: 'Ceiling' -> 'Floor'】

"Let's turn this plane over!"

Boom!

With a deafening roar, the entire world flipped.

The artificial gravity instantly reversed. Li Crazy and Su Qian, who were originally standing, "fell" towards the original ceiling. Rows of "passengers" screamed silently as they fell towards the new "floor", colliding like dominoes.

The "black mist" that was trying to invade from the walls was dispersed by the gravity shock.

Li Crazy used the reaction force to leap towards the cockpit door. He knew the "Captain" was there.

But just as his hand touched the door handle, he heard a soft "clack" sound from the floor (originally the carpet).

His peripheral vision caught it.

It was that piece of red Lego, thrown in the corner by him.

At this moment, the Lego block was flashing with a frantic red light, and the engraving on it was changing rapidly:

`[Scheme B Triggered: Sanity Critical! Host Identity Unstable! Suggesting... Immediate Execution of 'Crash' Plan!]`

Li Crazy's pupils constricted.

Su Qian's plan... the self-destruct plan...

He instinctively turned to look at Su Qian.

At this moment, Su Qian, bathed in the reversed gravity, her hair floating, her face pale but her expression resolute, didn't look at the cockpit, but at him.

Her thin lips moved, seemingly forming two words.

But the noise was too loud. Li Crazy couldn't hear it.

He only saw her eyes. In that pitch-black eye vortex, there was no madness, only a calm, almost terrifying determination.

And, deep love. A possessive, desperate love.

"You..."

Li Crazy's heart skipped a beat.

The Lego block in the corner suddenly jumped, transformed into a sharp spike, and shot straight towards Su Qian's back!

"Watch out!" Li Crazy roared, abandoning the cockpit door, twisting his body in the air to block the spike with his own back.

*Pfft!*

The spike pierced his shoulder, embedding itself into the flesh, missing the heart by a few centimeters.

Pain shot through his nerves.

But the Lego spike stopped attacking. It seemed to have misjudged the target, or rather, it was "stuck" by Li crazy's interference.

Li Crazy pulled out the spike, blood gushing out.

He looked at the Lego block in his hand, which had dimmed.

"Is this... your loyalty test?" He gasped, looking at Su Qian.

But Su Qian was looking at him in confusion, seemingly unaware of the Lego's "mutiny".

"Crazy? Your back..." She exclaimed, trying to rush over.

Li Crazy waved his hand, wiped the blood from his mouth, and grinned.

"Minor injury. Just... the 'autopilot' trying to take over."

He crushed the Lego block in his hand, turning it into powder.

"Don't worry. The only one allowed to crash this plane... is me."

He kicked open the cockpit door.

"Alright, Captain. Your tea party is over."

Inside the cockpit, there was no human pilot.

On the pilot's seat sat a giant, pulsating brain. Its surface was covered in eyes, each pupil reflecting the timeline of a different "Monday".

"Welcome..." the brain spoke directly into their minds, "To the... real... Monday."

At the same time, outside the window, the "blue sky" shattered. A giant clock face appeared in the sky, the second hand ticking backwards, moving towards the number "13".

The time... was going backwards.

Li Crazy's left eye widened.

"This isn't... the Old Day Corridor. This is..."

"The Recycling Bin." The brain laughed hoarsely. "We are all... deleted files."

【Warning! Detected 'Time Reversal' Field. The 'Logic' system is collapsing. Sanity is falling rapidly! Current...】

But he laughed. He laughed maniacally.

"Deleted files? Perfect."

"Since we are garbage, then let's see... who is the sharper piece of trash!"

"Su Qian! Don't look! Close your eyes!"

The final feast of the flight, began.
